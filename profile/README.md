## MerlinRx Technologies

A boutique healthcare SaaS company building modern infrastructure for hospice pharmacy.

[**merlinrx.info**](https://merlinrx.info)

### What we do

MerlinRx is a single, integrated platform for hospice pharmacy administration and electronic prescribing. We help hospice operators run pharmacy claims processing, formulary management, prior authorization, and ePrescribing in one system, with the data clinicians and administrators need surfaced exactly where decisions are made.

We are not a pharmacy. We are a technology company that sits between hospice providers and the pharmacy network, with interests aligned to the hospice rather than to pharmacy spend.

### What makes us different

- **Critical data at the critical moment.** Coverage, contract pricing, formulary standing, and prior authorization requirements appear directly inside the workflows where prescriptions and orders are written. No phone calls, no second screens, no separate reports.
- **Pass-through pricing.** We pass pharmacy contract rates to clients without markup. Every dollar your patients cost is a dollar you see.
- **One platform, not a stitched-together suite.** Orders, prescriptions, claims, formulary, and prior authorization live in a single integrated system, not a patchwork of vendors.
- **Built by hospice people.** The founding team has spent careers implementing hospice pharmacy systems, and we built MerlinRx around the workflows we know break.
- **Modern from the ground up.** Not a legacy platform with a new interface, MerlinRx was built with current technology and workflows designed around how people actually work today.

### Who we serve

Hospice organizations of all sizes, from independent community programs to large multi-site, multi-state operators. We are also pursuing Epic Connection Hub integration to support health system employed hospice programs.
